Ligand conformer generation

with RDKit & OpenBabel Confab


    • Ligand conformer generation is usually performed prior to (protein) ligand docking. OpenBabel Confab [1] generates low energy ligand conformers. RDKit [2] uses the ETKDG method. May not work for either too large or too small molecules. Three force field options are provided for Confab.

    [1] https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3073927/

    [2] https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.jcim.5b00654

    Avoid ambiguities in the PDB file, primarily in the atom name column and also the last atom column.
